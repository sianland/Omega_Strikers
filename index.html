Juliette

More Volver

Juliette was raised in the streets of Ahten city by her grandma. From a young age Juliette had one passion: to someday play in the Pro League alongside her idol, Estelle. As soon as she was old enough to smack the core, Juliette trained herself to be the best. She played in back-alley pickup leagues with strikers far her senior in the hopes that she could grow her skills more quickly. More than a few times that meant hobbling home, cut and bruised, after a tough match.

To help take care of her elderly grandma, Juliette took to working at the local soondubu shop. One day when talking out the trash, she discovered a small, hamster-like creature in the dumpster. She took the creature out and fed it some tofu. To her surprise, it couldn't get enough and ate block after block. She affectionately named him "Dubu" and the two became inseparable.

Dubu quickly grew to a massive size and joined Juliette on the Corestrike fields. Her fiery and relentless playstyle was complimented by Dubu's solid defense, and the two began to dominate the amateur leagues. Eventually, the rising duo caught the eye of a wealthy mogul looking to make the next great Pro League team. He saw Juliette's insatiable hunger and believed she had the potential to become the world's next big star.

Now, Juliette steps out onto the field alongside her heroes. But her goal is not merely to join the Pro League. She intends on becoming the best, no matter what it takes.

Estelle

More Volver

Estelle is one of the greatest strikers to ever play the game. Her effortless yet flashy playstyle allows her to control the flow of the game from afar, always one step ahead of her opponents.

She was the youngest player to ever claim a championship title and racked up accolades during her long career in the Pro League. While fans placed an immense pressure on Estelle to perform, it seemed there was nothin she couldn't do.

But from great heights come great falls. When Estelle was at the top of her game she was dethroned by a young upstart, Kai, who took the championship in a stunning series. In that moment, Estelle felt her passion for the game fade and went into retirement, turning her sights to other interests. She utilized her ubiquitous fame to become an iconic model - appearing in everything from billboards to blockbusters.

Even so, embers of Estelle's competitive spirit still burned inside her. She was approached by a wealthy mogul looking to start the next great pro league team and felt an irresistible pull to answer the call.

Estelle now returns to the Pro League alongside a team of adoring rookies who play with the pure love for the game she once had. All eyes are on her, and given her past, the public pressure to perform on the field remains as intense as it ever was.

But this time around it feels different - the demands of the crowd aren't her focus. Altough she's outwardly as cool and collected as always, Estelle can feel the old desire to win burning once again.

Dubu

More Volver

Big, round, and ready to hold the line against whatever the opposing team may throw at him, Dubu is the friend-shaped defender that every striker wants on their team. His ability to block off large portions of the arena is unmatched.

Dubu's origins are unclear. Juliette icon.png Juliette found him as a small creature, shuffling around for food in a back-alley dumpster. She took him inside a soondubu restaurant owned by her grandmother's friend and fed him a block of tofu. He scarfed the block up and Juliette happily fed him more, and more, and more. She affectionately named him "Dubu", after the tofu he loved to eat.

Over time, Dubu grew to massive size, soon towering over Juliette. He followed Juliette everywhere she went - waiting outside of her classrooms at school, helping at her work at the soondubu shop, and playing alongside her on the Corestrike fields. Dubu took to his role as Juliette's protector, defending her from older kids that would try and bully her, steal her equipment, or beat her up on the field. Dubu viewed this as a small token of appreciation for everything Juliette had done for him.

Together, the two grew strong and rose up the ladders of the amateur leagues. Their ascension was noticed by a business mogul looking to make the next great Pro League team. He made Juliette an offer to join his team, but she wasn't going anywhere without Dubu. Reluctantly, the mogul agreed and put the two on a team together with Estelle icon.png Estelle. Dubu is excited to help Juliette accomplish her dream of becoming a Pro League champion, but deep down he wonders if he will ever learn more about who he is and where he comes from.

Kai

More Volver

Kai's a stunning hotshot whose raw speed on the field is only matched by his own confidence. His kit allows him to skirmish from a distance or pressure up close when the opportunity arises.

By the time he turned fifteen, Kai had already stepped into the Pro Leagues with every expectation that he would follow in his father's footsteps and sweep away the competition. However, to the world's surprise; Kai struggled. At the close of his rookie season: he failed to overcome the long-time champion, Estelle, in the finals. Kai was stunned and, even worse, his father's disappointment was immeasurable.

Kai called upon his childhood friend, Era, to join his team and in the following season; they dethroned Estelle. They went on to dominate the Pro League, winning championship after championship. Kai became an icon of success around the world - his face emblazoning every billboard and his name placed on every product from sports drinks to shoes.

Even with this streak of dominance, Kai could tell his father wasn't satisfied. Kai knew that to meet his expectations, he'd have to be seen as the greatest player of all time, surpassing even the reputation of his father.

That opportunity may yet present itself - the Pro League looks to be stacked with the most formidable competition it's ever had! Old rivals and new challengers approach, each bringing with them an opportunity for Kai to prove himself. Perhaps in the coming seasons, Kai will get the challenge he seeks.

Hailing from a family of professional strikers, Kai was molded into becoming one of the greatest strikers in history and to be anything but the best is to be a failure in their family. To meet his father's demanding expectations, Kai trained relentlessly from a young age, facing off against professional players at just ten years old.
